# SageAttention2: Efficient Attention with Thorough Outlier Smoothing and Per-thread INT4 Quantization

**Authors**: Jintao Zhang, Haofeng Huang, Pengle Zhang, Jia Wei, Jun Zhu, Jianfei Chen  
**Affiliation**: Tsinghua University; Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences  
**Venue**: ICML 2025  
**arXiv**: 2411.10958

## Abstract Summary

Extends SageAttention from INT8 to INT4/FP8. Quantizes Q,K to INT4 via per-thread quantization; P to FP8 with V in FP8. Three innovations:
- (C1) INT4 range [-7,+7] too narrow for outliers → per-thread quantization (groups tokens by GPU thread, single quantization scale per thread, zero dequantization overhead) + Q-smoothing (subtract per-block mean)
- (C2) FP8 accumulator for P̃V Matmul is actually FP22 → two-level accumulation (FP32 buffer after each P-block Matmul)
- Optional V-smoothing (subtract V mean, add back)

## Key Results

- 481 TOPS on RTX4090, 3× FlashAttention2, 4.5× xformers
- SageAttention2-8b variant (INT8 Q,K) matches FlashAttention3(fp8) speed on Hopper GPUs at higher accuracy
- Near-zero end-to-end metrics loss on language, image, video generation models
