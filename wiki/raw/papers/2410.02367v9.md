# SageAttention: Accurate 8-bit Attention for Plug-and-Play Inference Acceleration

**Authors**: Jintao Zhang, Jia Wei, Haofeng Huang, Pengle Zhang, Jun Zhu, Jianfei Chen  
**Affiliation**: Tsinghua University (BNRist / THBI Lab)  
**Venue**: ICLR 2025  
**arXiv**: 2410.02367

## Abstract Summary

Quantizes Q,K to INT8 and handles P,V in FP16 with FP16 accumulator. Addresses two challenges:
- (C1) Channel-wise outliers in K → smooth K by subtracting per-channel mean
- (C2) Accuracy of P,V product → keep P,V in FP16 using FP16-with-FP16-accumulator (fast on RTX4090/3090)

Per-block quantization aligned with FlashAttention tiling. Drop-in replacement, no model modification needed.

## Key Results

- 2.1× over FlashAttention2, 2.7× over xformers in kernel OPS
- 340 TOPS on RTX4090 at headdim=64
- Near-zero end-to-end accuracy loss across LLMs, image generation, video generation
- Code: https://github.com/thu-ml/SageAttention
