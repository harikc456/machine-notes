# SageAttention3: Microscaling FP4 Attention for Inference and An Exploration of 8-bit Training

**Authors**: Jintao Zhang, Jia Wei, Haoxu Wang, Pengle Zhang, Xiaoming Xu, Haofeng Huang, Kai Jiang, Jianfei Chen, Jun Zhu  
**Affiliation**: Tsinghua University; Shengshu Tech.  
**Venue**: NeurIPS 2025  
**arXiv**: 2505.11594

## Abstract Summary

Two contributions:

**1. FP4 inference (SageAttention3)**:  
Uses NVFP4 (E2M1) microscaling with 1×16 group size. Applies FP4 to both Q×Kᵀ and P×V Matmuls. Two-level quantization for P̃ matrix (narrow [0,1] range creates inefficient E4M3 scale factors — first normalize per-token to [0, 448×6], then apply FP4). Inherits K-smoothing and Q-smoothing from SageAttention/2.
- 1038 TOPS on RTX5090 (Blackwell FP4 Tensor Cores), 5× FlashAttention2 on same GPU

**2. 8-bit training (SageBwd)**:  
First trainable low-bit attention. Identifies most accuracy-sensitive backward Matmul and keeps it in FP16; applies 8-bit to others.
- Fine-tuning: lossless performance convergence
- Pretraining: slower convergence (not recommended yet)
